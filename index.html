About Us

Shree Media Research & Survey is engaged in a wide range of activities since 1995.

The range encompasses conducting opinion polls, feedback surveys, critical analysis of high profile politicians, and many other political services.

We have successfully conducted opinion poll surveys and feedback surveys in Assembly constituencies of Maharashtra & across the states.

Many Assembly constituencies in Gujarat, Rajasthan, Goa, and Karnataka have been covered by us successfully.

  • Opinion poll surveys for a number of municipal corporations, councils, and self-government bodies in Maharashtra.
  • Accuracy mark above 95% in all cases.
  • Trusted by recognized political parties and personalities.

Our Services

Random Survey

We cover major areas in your constituency and take the pulse of voters. The major areas like taluka panchayat, nagarpanchayat, or wards are covered in this survey.

Deep Steek

Each and every part of your constituency is covered. We'll give you exact feedback of your current position which indeed will help you in deciding the strategy.

Monitoring

Our representatives will constantly be in touch with voters for 15 - 30 days and will give you daily updates on the constituency's political scenario.

Personality Development

We will study and examine the constituency in depth and will consult you regarding your position, opposition's current status, political status of the constituency, and how you can make your next move land in an appropriate manner.

Whisper Campaign

Our trained representatives will be in your constituency a few days before the election and will spread positive points for you and negative points about your opposition, which will help to set a trend in your favor.
