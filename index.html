About Us

Shree Media Research & Survey is engaged in a wide range of activities since 1995.

The range encompasses conducting opinion polls, feedback surveys, critical analysis of high profile politicians, and many other political services.

We have successfully conducted opinion poll surveys and feedback surveys in Assembly constituencies of Maharashtra & across the states.

Many Assembly constituencies in Gujarat, Rajasthan, Goa, and Karnataka have been covered by us successfully.

  • Opinion poll surveys for a number of municipal corporations, councils, and self-government bodies in Maharashtra.
  • Accuracy mark above 95% in all cases.
  • Trusted by recognized political parties and personalities.
